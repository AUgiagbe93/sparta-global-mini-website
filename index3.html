I create Art.

I am a self-taught artist, who specializes in Cartoon/Comic Art styles and Character design.

My inspirations include Hiroyuki Takei, Alex Ahad, Masashi Kishimoto and Hiroyuki Imaishi.

Tools of the Trade include:

  • Programs: Clip Studio Paint, Paint Tool SAI, Adobe Photoshop CS6, Aseprite

  • Hardware: Wacom Intuos Pen & Touch Medium
